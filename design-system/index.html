01 · Palette

Green is the voice. Orange is the whisper.

Nectar grounds every page; Seaweed is the ink. Dark Foliage carries the brand — buttons, footer, full-bleed statements. Smoky Orange appears at most once per view. Khaki lives on dark grounds only.

02 · Typography

La Luxes speaks. Garet works.

La Luxes Serif draws every glyph as a capital — statements set themselves in caps, so keep them short. It speaks only in headlines. Garet carries every working surface: navigation, body, buttons, captions — uppercase and wide-tracked at small sizes. 03 · Signature — the Hemline

A line that moves like a hem.

The recurring device of the whole site: a one-pixel line that catches air on interaction — link underlines lift into a curve, and section dividers drape gently. It is the only ornament the system allows. Hover the links below.

04 · Motion — the Drape

Everything settles like fabric.

Two easings only: silk for reveals, drape for weighted moves. Content enters bottom-up, the way a hem falls. Scroll reveals below run once; reduced-motion users see everything immediately.
