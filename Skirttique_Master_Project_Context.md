> Comprehensive project context for building the Skirttique website with

## Brand Overview

**Brand Name:** Skirttique

**Tagline:** Defined by Elegance. Rooted in Modesty.

Skirttique is a premium, luxury modest fashion brand dedicated
exclusively to **midi and maxi skirts**. The brand exists to redefine
skirts as timeless wardrobe essentials rather than basic clothing items.

Although Nigerian-owned, Skirttique is positioned as a **global luxury
fashion house** targeting customers in Nigeria, South Africa, the UK,
the US and the Middle East.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Vision

To become a globally recognized fashion house known for redefining
